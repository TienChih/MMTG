Definition 2.4.1.

A finite projective plane of order \(n\) (\(n\geq 2\)) is a model that satisfies the Axioms Axiom 2.2.5, Axiom 2.2.6, Axiom 2.2.7, Axiom 2.2.21 and the following additional Axioms:
Axiom 2.4.2.
Every pair of distinct lines intersect at at least one point.
Axiom 2.4.3.
There is at least one line \(L\) containing exactly \(n+1\) points.
